◆ InteractableSelectMode

enum UnityEngine.XR.Interaction.Toolkit.InteractableSelectMode

Options for the selection policy of an Interactable.

See also
IXRSelectInteractable.selectMode
Enumerator
Single 

Allows the Interactable to only be selected by a single Interactor at a time and allows other Interactors to take selection by automatically deselecting.

Multiple 

Allows for multiple Interactors at a time to select the Interactable.

This option can be disabled in the Inspector window by adding the CanSelectMultipleAttribute with a value of false to a derived class of XRBaseInteractable.

◆ MatchOrientation

enum UnityEngine.XR.Interaction.Toolkit.MatchOrientation

The option of which object's orientation in the rig Unity matches with the destination after teleporting.

Enumerator
WorldSpaceUp 

After teleporting the XR Origin will be positioned such that its up vector matches world space up.

TargetUp 

After teleporting the XR Origin will be positioned such that its up vector matches target up.

TargetUpAndForward 

After teleporting the XR Origin will be positioned such that its up and forward vectors match target up and forward, respectively.

None 

After teleporting the XR Origin will not attempt to match any orientation.
